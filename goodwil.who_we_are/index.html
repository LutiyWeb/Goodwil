Who we are?

Goodwill Capital is a fund created by a team of investment professionals with over 25 years of experience in venture capital and private equity.

We are proud to offer our clients:

Advanced and creative solutions

Our clients receive the best investment solutions and products, taking into account current trends and based on the fund's professional team’s many years of experience.

World-class service provider partnerships.

We work with the best auditors, lawyers, underwriters and brokers to help our clients take less risk, earn more, grow and achieve their goals.

World class team.

Together we are a strong, global team of like-minded people, inspired to deliver great results.

ORGANIZATION CHART

Goodwill Capital is registered in Canada as a Limited Partnership (LP).

The structure of the fund includes general partners (GP) and limited partners (LP).

General Partners (GP)

Investment professionals who are responsible for decision making on financing projects and any company transactions. They manage the business and take over all operational activities

Limited Partners (LP)

Provide financing for the company and participate in its management
