FOR INVESTORS

We offer opportunities for private investors to take part in the formation of sponsorship capital in promising
SPAC projects.

COOPERATION SCHEME
step 1

Conclusion of a contract

To consolidate the partnership, it is necessary to conclude an investment agreement between the investor and Goodwill Capital.

Under the agreement, the investor receives the status of a limited partner (LP) and the opportunity to receive income from the fund's activities proportional to their contribution.

step 2

Investment

An investment can be made in two ways:

Bank transfer (in USD)

Bitcoin transfer (at the exchange rate against US dollars at the time the funds are received by the fund)

step 3

SPAC funding and listing

After the formation of an investment pool for SPAC sponsorship, Goodwill Capital contributes sponsored capital and receives its shares equal to 11% of the total (at this stage, the fund triples the capital).

On average, this process takes up to 3 months.

step 4

Buying a target company

In accordance with the SPAC roadmap, a target company is searched for and bought. As this is a positive informational occasion, the value of shares automatically increases.

On average, this process takes 3-5 months.

step 5

Fixing the fund's profit

Depending on various factors, such as the quality of the target сompany, the qualifications of the founding team, etc., the fund makes the best decision on profit fixing. As part of this decision, the shares owned by the fund will be sold.

On average, this happens 9 to 12 months after SPAC's IPO.

step 6

Profit distribution

In accordance with the investment agreement, the profit is distributed between the fund and the investor.

The resulting profit (together with the initial investment) can be withdrawn to a bank account, bitcoin wallet, or reinvested in the SPAC at the investor's decision.
